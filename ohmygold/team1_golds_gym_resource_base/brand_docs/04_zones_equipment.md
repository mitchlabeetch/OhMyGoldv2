## 1. Overview

Gold's Gym France clubs feature **seven distinct training zones**, each designed for specific fitness disciplines. The zones reflect the brand's dual heritage: serious bodybuilding tradition combined with modern, inclusive fitness approaches.

### Zone Philosophy

The training spaces are designed with an **arena mentality** -- each zone frames training as combat, challenge, and personal triumph:

> *"Bienvenue dans l'arene. Ici, c'est toi contre la gravite, un combat a chaque rep, une victoire a chaque drop."*

## 8. Musculation Guidee -- Guided Machine Weights

### Description
The guided strength training area features selectorized and plate-loaded machines for controlled, targeted muscle development.

### Equipment
- **Selectorized machines** -- Pin-loaded weight stacks
- **Plate-loaded machines** -- Free-weight resistance
- **Cable stations** -- Adjustable pulley systems
- **Smith machines** -- Guided barbell movement
- **Leg press** -- Horizontal and 45-degree variants
- **Chest press** -- Seated and incline options
- **Lat pulldown** -- Wide-grip and close-grip
- **Rowing machines** -- Seated cable rows
- **Shoulder press** -- Overhead pressing station
- **Bicep curl** -- Preacher curl and machine curl
- **Tricep extension** -- Pushdown and overhead stations
- **Leg extension** -- Quadricep isolation
- **Leg curl** -- Hamstring isolation
- **Calf raise** -- Standing and seated variants
- **Abductor/adductor** -- Inner/outer thigh
- **Abdominal crunch** -- Core isolation machines

### Target Users
- Beginners learning proper form
- Members recovering from injury
- Bodybuilders seeking isolation work
- Anyone wanting controlled movement patterns
