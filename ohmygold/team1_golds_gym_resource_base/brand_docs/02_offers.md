## 1. Subscription Tiers Overview

> **Note on Coaching/PT Sessions:** Personal training and coaching sessions are **NOT** included in any base membership tier. They are available as separate add-on purchases. This is verified by the subscription page structure on goldsgymfrance.fr where coaching is listed separately from membership benefits.

Gold's Gym France offers **three subscription tiers**: Basic, Flex, and Premium. All tiers are offered **without long-term commitment** but require an **8-week notice period** for cancellation.

## 2. Detailed Tier Breakdown

### Basic Tier -- EUR 7.99/week

> *The entry point for serious fitness.*

**Included Features:**
- Full access to the national Gold's Gym France network (all clubs)
- Home training via the Gold's Gym France app
- Training and progress tracking through the app
- International access (Gold's Gym global network)
- QR code-based club entry via mobile app

**Not Included:**
- Hydromassage
- Body composition analysis
- Premium amenities (Sportwatter, boutique discounts)
- Group classes (Hyrox, MMA)

**Best For:** Budget-conscious fitness enthusiasts who want core gym access with digital tracking.

---

### Flex Tier -- EUR 9.99/week

> *The balanced choice with wellness perks.*

**Included Features (Basic +):**
- Hydromassage access
- Body composition analysis scale
- Sportwatter (6 flavors) -- flavored hydration station
- International access
- Everything in Basic tier

**Not Included:**
- Shareable membership card
- Boutique discount
- Hyrox and MMA group classes

**Best For:** Members who want wellness amenities and recovery features alongside their training.

---

### Premium Tier -- EUR 10.99/week

> *Best seller. The complete experience.*

**Included Features (Flex +):**
- Shareable membership card (1 member of same household, non-simultaneous use)
- 10% discount on club boutique year-round
- Hyrox and MMA group training classes
- Everything in Flex tier

**Best For:** Serious fitness enthusiasts, couples/households, and those who want group classes and retail perks.

## 5. Enrollment Process

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Choose your club** -- Select between Thiais or Val d'Europe
2. **Select your tier** -- Basic, Flex, or Premium
3. **Complete health questionnaire** -- Declare fitness capability
4. **Accept terms and conditions** -- Check acceptance box online
5. **Provide payment details** -- Set up direct debit (prelevement automatique)
6. **Download the app** -- Generate QR code for club access
7. **Receive confirmation** -- Membership activated immediately

### Required Documentation
- Proof of age (18+ required)
- Valid payment method (bank account or card)
- Health declaration (self-certified)
- Photo (for member profile)

## 6. Cancellation Policy (Detailed)

### Standard Cancellation

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Notice Period** | 8 weeks minimum |
| **Method** | Via member portal on goldsgymfrance.fr |
| **Payment During Notice** | Member must pay all subscription fees during the 8-week period |
| **Re-enrollment** | Possible anytime; must pay enrollment fee + outstanding amounts |

### Medical Cancellation
- Must be notified by **registered mail (courrier recommande)**
- Requires **medical certificate (avis medical)**
- Effective upon receipt of documentation
- No penalty with valid medical justification

### 14-Day Cooling-Off Period (Right of Withdrawal)
- All online subscriptions have a **14-day withdrawal period**
- Full refund of amounts paid within 14 days of notification
- Same payment method used for purchase will be used for refund
- Standard withdrawal form provided in contract appendix

### Payment Incident Penalties

| Incident | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|
| 1st payment failure | EUR 8 penalty fee |
| 2nd consecutive failure | Immediate termination by registered mail |
| Card pre-authorization | Up to 2 months of membership held as guarantee |

## 7. Terms & Conditions Highlights

### Membership Rules
- **Personal & non-transferable** -- Cannot be assigned to another person
- **QR code required** for all club access (generated via app)
- **Age requirement:** 18+ years only
- **Enrollment fee** is a one-time payment for the duration of membership
- **Re-enrollment** requires paying current enrollment fee again

### Club Rules
- Respect and proper treatment of all members and staff is mandatory
- Aggressive, insulting, or inappropriate behavior results in immediate expulsion and membership termination
- Prohibition of performance-enhancing drugs (anabolic steroids) -- immediate termination and possible legal action
- Proper use of equipment required; inappropriate use is member's responsibility
- Daily lockers provided free of charge; overnight use prohibited
- Gold's Gym is not responsible for personal items left unsecured

### Price Changes
- Clubs may modify tariffs at any time with prior notice
- Members may cancel within 8 weeks if prices increase
- Old tariff remains applicable during the notice period
- Promotional pricing is subject to change

### Liability
- Gold's Gym is **not responsible** for health issues resulting from exercise
- Members must consult a physician before starting exercise (especially with hypertension, angina, heart disease, diabetes, chronic illness)
- Personal items are member's responsibility unless stored in properly closed lockers
- Equipment misuse liability falls on the member
